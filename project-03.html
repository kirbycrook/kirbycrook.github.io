JOKULA

JOKULA is an advertising agency in Reykjavik, Iceland. They specialize in branding, web design, development, marketing, and other services. As a designer for JOKULA, I primarily designed user experiences for various web and mobile applications.

Krauma is a geothermal hotspring resort located in southern Iceland. I served as a consultant for the website design, making any last adjustments before the site went live. I also designed the booking system and dashboard for the company admin to track guests, check-ins, and pool capacity.
