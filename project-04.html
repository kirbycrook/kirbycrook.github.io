JOKULA

Another project for JOKULA involved designing an operating budgeting software program to be used by various clients. Due to specific budgeting rules, some steps in the user experience were required to stick to industry standards. The end result was a dashboard that diplays a comparison between industry averages and the company budget, announcements, a forum, and the ability to record and edit the budget.
