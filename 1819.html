1819

1819 is a database and phone directory for the country of Iceland. During my tenure with Jokula, I was the UX lead for the project. My role included preliminary research, analytics tracking, site mapping, and wireframing, among other responsibilities. Prior to the development of the products, I pitched the designs and rationale to a panel of executives from 1819.

Due to non-disclosure agreements, further details on process and design-thinking cannot be published.
